Mission Statement

As a costume and set designer particularly interested in textiles, I am intrigued by the ways that textures and tones can make both the performer and audience feel the characters, settings, mood of the scene on their skin, enabling them to immerse themselves more deeply in the theatrical experience. My work is concerned with how and when tactility can be used effectively, while considering the ethical issues that can potentially arise when immersing audiences in this way.

With the influences of Louise Bourgeois, Fritz Lang, Michael Clark, Eiko Ishioka and Es Devlin, I draw on a background in fine art and textiles to inspire my practice. Working best under time constraints and the excitement of live performance, and as a designer, I am open to fresh challenges in other mediums including film making and exhibitions.

Working as a creative duo with Inigo Townsend, we devise productions from scratch including the design of the set & costume to lighting, props, and direction. Our main purpose is centred within immersive experiences to provide creative direction, both in collaboration with creatives and original ideas. We are leaving CSM with a performance company and have secured our first commission as art directors of a commercial immersive theatre experience.
