Pleasance

“The Space Project” was held during the national lockdown in February 2020. This project was led by Anna and Alice from the Bric a Brac theatre company. I was very happy to be included in this project as it was only meant for the MA Performance: Design and Practice students. Only four BA students were allowed onto the project and Inigo and I made it. Based on the 1992 STS-47 NASA Space Shuttle mission.



A week before we started the project, the directors, Anna and Alice sent stimuli image/poem/song/film clip to base a 3 minute performance on. This is the image I was sent along with two other students Inigo and Giulia.



This is a still image from our three minute performance that we had a week to prepare and then perform on Monday the 22nd of February.



This was an intense week-long workshop that moved very quickly. It was based off of the lessons that the directors Anna and Alice learned at the School Jacques Lecoq. The day after we did our 3-minute performance we were given the brief for the workshop task. We would create a 10-minute mixed media live performance based on the 'STS 47 Launch Flight and Landing Recap’ on YouTube and the aftermath of the launch. The project was based on the 1992 STS-47 NASA Space Shuttle mission. Onboard were Mae Jemison, the first African American Woman in Space, and Jan Davis who had secretly married her partner and co-pilot Mark Lee just a month before the launch. On their return instead of questions about their work as astronauts and a celebration of their achievements the world was interested in only one thing: whether or not this couple had sex.



Image 4 is a still of the 10-minute performance we made. It began in the style of an expository documentary style, them became increasingly more surreal as the play went on. Using a live vision mixer, live feed projection and diegetic sound effects.
