The Keeper



The Keeper is a short film that was created as part of a horror event at the National Film and Television School called ‘Gorefest’. As part of the creative team, I designed, sourced, and made the costumes, working closely with the Art Director Jack Haggerty.

The short film explores concepts around mental health, themes of loss and afterlife: telling the story of a murdering lighthouse keeper who has lost her entire family and lives alone who attempts to turn Sam, her sailor victim into a merman.
