Janet

In April 2021, a friend recommended Inigo and I to Darren Julien of Julien’s Auctions, a privately held auction house in Culver City. Julien hired us to curate the window display at the Hard Rock Café in London for an auction of Janet Jackson’s costumes from her tour and from some of her music videos.

The challenge of this project was that we had to work from photographic images and use these to capture and piece together in a single display the essence of Janet’s Jackson’s style. Usually when I style garments, I follow my own feelings about what would go with what. In this task, we couldn’t mix and match the pieces but had to arrange and accessorise them in a marketable, photogenic way for the press and potential buyers.

From working on this project, I learned about costume for large-scale musical performances. The looks that were created for stage required very specific details that were adapted for the stage setting. As Janet was a dancer, there were knee pads custom made for many of her trousers and microphone pockets sewn into the bodice of her dresses.
